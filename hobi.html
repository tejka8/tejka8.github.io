Fitness

How did it became my hobby?

It first started as a weight loss journey in 2020, but as time passed it grew into a habit and then it kind of became a hobby. The first couple of years I did body weight exercises at home, following exercising and nutrition plans. This helped me to lose 10 kilograms in just a year. After reaching my goal (weight wise) I started making my own nutrition plans to help me gain muscles. In May 2023, I started implementing weights in my workouts and the goal for me is by the end of this year to start weight lifting.

The importance of warmup & streching...

  • Injury prevention
  • Increases flexibility
  • It prepares the muscles for more intense training
  • Helps in muscle recovery
  • Improved circulation

Tips for consistency

  • Acknowledge the discomfort
  • Find a workout and nutrition plan that works for you
  • Set micro goals
  • Find a workout friend
  • Pick the same time of day to work out
